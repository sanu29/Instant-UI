Alerts
An alert is machine-to-person communication that is important or time sensitive. It an automated message or notification which indicates that a predefined event or error condition has occurred and that some action is needed or done.
Simple Alerts
For Simple Alerts just use alert class and alert keyword followed by the -variant names.
