Modals And Rating
Modals are the key to turning leads into conversions. The key is not to overdo and fill your webpage with them, and design them effectively so the user can feel like they are part of the main page while addressing the important message they have to read in the newer smaller window.

The fact that you provide ratings on your site creates trust and benefits you as web master, even if you get negative feedback.
